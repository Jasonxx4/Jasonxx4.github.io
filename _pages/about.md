<section id="about-me" class="home-section home-about">
  <p class="home-lead"><strong>I work on federated graph learning, large language models, and spatiotemporal data mining.</strong></p>
  <p>Currently, I am a Ph.D. student at the University of Technology Sydney under the supervision of <a href="https://hanchen-wang.com/">Prof. Hanchen Wang</a> and <a href="[https://hanchen-wang.com](https://profiles.uts.edu.au/lu.qin)/">Prof. Lu Qin</a>.</p>
  <p>Before my Ph.D. studies, I received the M.S. degree from Zhejiang University of Technology and the B.Sc. degree from China Jiliang University. I have published four papers in machine learning and data mining, including WWW, CIKM, Information Sciences, and IEEE Internet of Things Journal.</p>
</section>
