<section id="about-me" class="home-section home-about">
  <p class="home-lead"><strong>I work on federated graph learning, large language models, and spatiotemporal data mining.</strong></p>
  <p>Currently, I am pursuing the M.S. degree under the advisory of Prof. Guojiang Shen and Prof. Xiangjie Kong in the College of Computer Science and Technology at Zhejiang University of Technology, Zhejiang, China.</p>
  <p>Before my Master's studies, I received the B.Sc. degree from China Jiliang University. I have published three papers in machine learning and data mining, including CIKM, Information Sciences, and IEEE Internet of Things Journal.</p>
